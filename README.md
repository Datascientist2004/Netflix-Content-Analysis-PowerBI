# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Netflix content data to uncover trends, patterns, and business insights.  
An interactive dashboard was built to explore content distribution, genres, ratings, and release trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze Netflix content trends over time  
- Identify most popular genres and categories  
- Understand country-wise content distribution  
- Provide insights for business decision-making  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- 📈 Content on Netflix increased significantly after 2015  
- 🎭 Drama and Comedy are the most dominant genres  
- 🌍 USA contributes the highest number of titles  
- 🎬 Movies are more than TV Shows on the platform  
- 📅 Majority of content added in recent years
